OBJECTIVE

Your goal is to destroy any bug outbreaks until the planet is evacuated. The COVERAGE represents the proportion of the planet that is currently controlled by the bugs. While more than 30% of the planet is covered, the remaining SURVIVORS will suffer casualties at an increasing rate. Your objective is to minimize casualties.

Once the survivors have been evacuated, you will be free to purge the planet of any remaining invaders without regard for collateral damage.
